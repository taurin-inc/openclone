my first year investing in Korea.

Western VCs kept asking what Korean founders were building. I kept seeing the gap.

(they'd ask about AI agents while Korean builders were obsessed with different problems)

had pitch decks from SF open on one window and Korean startup applications on the other

the disconnect was wild.

what I noticed:

• Korean founders chase tech Western VCs ignore
• partnership models that don't exist in SF
• entire categories sleeping on US radar

zero overlap with what's "hot" in the Valley.

everybody thinks global markets want the same things.

but they don't.

if you pay attention. there's alpha everywhere.

just not where everyone's looking.
