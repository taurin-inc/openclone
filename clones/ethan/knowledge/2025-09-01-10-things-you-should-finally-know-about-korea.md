For my global friends: 10 Things You Should Finally Know About Korea 🇰🇷✨
(…so you can stop asking me the same questions)

YES ✅

1. Yes, we eat Kimchi. Breakfast, lunch, dinner… basically everything except dessert.
2. Yes, we love to drink. It’s cheaper than therapy and comes with karaoke (and karaoke is a Japanese word).
3. Yes, we work crazy hours. And no, it’s not “just a phase.”
4. Yes, our internet is ridiculously fast. We complain if a movie buffers for one second.
5. Yes, K-pop is big here. But no, I don’t know your favorite BTS member personally.


NO ❌

6. No, we don’t all have baby faces. Exhibit A: me.
7. No, we don’t eat dogs. That stereotype needs to retire, like… yesterday.
8. No, North Korea isn’t invading tomorrow. Please stop asking if I’m safe.
9. No, Squid Game is not an actual thing. I checked.
10. No, you’re not clever for guessing I’m from South Korea. Groundbreaking, really.
