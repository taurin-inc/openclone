Early-stage founders who built from zero with no perfect conditions, no prior knowledge, just pure execution and learning on the fly?

Here's what that actually looks like:

🔨 First 6 months: everything breaks, you fix it, repeat
🧠 Learning: Google searches at 2am, trial and error, asking dumb questions
💰 Resources: whatever you can scrape together, no safety net
📈 Growth: slow as hell, then suddenly not

Reality check: it's messy, uncomfortable, and nothing like the highlight reels.
