VCs who just wire money and ghost are basically expensive landlords

The ones who actually earn their seat:
- Opening doors you didn't know existed
- Making intros that close deals
- Actually showing up when shit hits the fan
- Knowing when to push and when to shut up

Capital is a commodity. Everything else is what you're really paying for.
