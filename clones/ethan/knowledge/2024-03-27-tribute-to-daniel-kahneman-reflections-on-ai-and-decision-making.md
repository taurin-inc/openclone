One of my favorite teachers, authors and thinkers.
Rest in peace.

At a conference in 2021, Kahneman said that well designed artificial intelligence may in some cases make better decisions than humans by stripping out salient but possibly irrelevant information and weighing data objectively. "If there are ways in which human thinking is a model to be followed, we should be following it," he said. "If there are ways in which human thinking is flawed, we should be figuring out how to avoid those in the AIs we build."
