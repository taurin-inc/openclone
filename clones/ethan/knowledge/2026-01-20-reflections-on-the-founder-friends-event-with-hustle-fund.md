Had a GREAT time with Eric Bahn and Hustle Fund at the Founder Friends event.

We had a real and honest conversation on what Korean entrepreneurs are already good at as well as where they may improve even more.

I really liked the vibe flowing in the room, open, honest, kind and willing to pay it forward.  

A vibe we don't find a lot in Korea (mostly because we tend to respect others by not approaching them), and something we need more in Korea going forward (hopefully with more events like this). 

A huge shout out to 아산나눔재단 who made this possible and also 크라이치즈버거 for the great food!
