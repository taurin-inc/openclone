## "Alarmy gave me a reason to wake up."

As the world’s No.1 alarm app, **Alarmy** has received over 2 million user reviews on the Apple App Store and Google Play, maintaining an impressive average rating of 4.8 stars.

We’ve selected some of the most heartfelt reviews from our users to share with you.

> Hello, I work as a nurse at a university hospital. Due to the nature of my job, I work 3 shifts, and since my schedule isn’t fixed and I have to strictly stick to certain times, the app is incredibly useful for me. (I’ve been using the app since my university days)
>
> But a few weeks ago? I started seeing messages pop up with every alarm I set. Today, I saw a message like “Just give up if you get lost?” and was surprised. I had only just started working, and every morning I felt so depressed, but these messages gave me so much strength. I really appreciated it, so I wanted to share my feedback like this.ㅎㅎ I usually don’t write things like this.. Thank you so much~ Every time I see a message when I wake up, it gives me strength.
