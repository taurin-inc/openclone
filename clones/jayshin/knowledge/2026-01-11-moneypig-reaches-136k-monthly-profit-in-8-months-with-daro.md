# MoneyPig reaches $136k monthly profit in 8 months with DARO

**Author:** DARO  
**Date:** Not specified

### Case Study: MoneyPig (The Wave)

MoneyPig is a popular South Korean "reward app" (often referred to as App Tech) developed by the company 'The Wave'. The app allows users to earn points by completing various tasks and missions, which can then be exchanged for cash or vouchers. 

#### The Challenge: Stagnant Revenue and Operational Burden
Despite having a solid user base and high engagement, the MoneyPig team faced significant obstacles in scaling their business:

*   **Operational Overload:** The small development team spent excessive time manually managing various ad networks and waterfall settings.
*   **Low eCPM:** Without specialized knowledge in ad bidding and mediation optimization, the app's ad revenue per thousand impressions (eCPM) remained lower than industry benchmarks.
*   **Scaling Difficulty:** High User Acquisition (UA) costs made it difficult to grow profitably because the lifetime value (LTV) of a user wasn't high enough to cover the marketing spend.

#### The DARO Solution: Full-Service Ad Monetization
DARO stepped in to take over the entire ad monetization workflow, allowing the MoneyPig team to focus on product development. Key actions included:

1.  **Automated Waterfall Optimization:** DARO implemented its proprietary technology to manage mediation in real-time, ensuring that the highest-paying ad inventory was always prioritized across multiple global ad networks.
2.  **Strategic Ad Placement:** DARO analyzed user flow to introduce new ad formats (such as rewarded video and interstitials) at high-conversion points without negatively impacting user retention.
3.  **Data Transparency:** By providing clear, unified data reports, DARO enabled the team to see the direct correlation between their marketing efforts and ad revenue.

#### The Results: Explosive Growth
The partnership led to a dramatic turnaround in the app's financial performance within less than a year:

*   **Monthly Profit Milestone:** Within 8 months of partnering with DARO, MoneyPig reached a monthly profit of **200 million KRW (approximately $136,000 USD)**.
*   **Increased eCPM:** Through aggressive mediation management and premium ad source integration, the eCPM saw a significant percentage increase compared to previous internal management.
*   **Operational Freedom:** The Wave team saved dozens of hours per week that were previously spent on ad operations, redirecting those resources toward improving app features and user experience.

#### Conclusion
The success of MoneyPig demonstrates that even for apps with high traffic, professional ad monetization management is crucial to maximizing profitability. By delegating ad operations to DARO, MoneyPig was able to scale its profit margins to a level that supported sustainable, long-term growth.
