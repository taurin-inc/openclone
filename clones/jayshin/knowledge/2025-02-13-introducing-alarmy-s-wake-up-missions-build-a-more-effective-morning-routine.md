# Introducing Alarmy's Wake-Up Missions: Build a More Effective Morning Routine

**Date:** 2025-02-13  
**Author:** (재)행복나눔재단 SIT(Social Innovators Table)팀

If you often find yourself turning off your alarm only to fall back asleep, a regular alarm might not be enough. **Alarmy's wake-up missions** go beyond just ringing an alarm—they actively engage your body and mind, ensuring you wake up completely.

In this article, we’ll introduce **Alarmy’s most popular wake-up missions** and explain the benefits of each one.

## **1. Photo mission** 📸

![Photo mission](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6748851ec062625f98012b8e/67acaa4aa423443cb7f115de_1000015196.jpg)

### ✅ **How does it work?**

To turn off the alarm, you must take a photo of a pre-registered location (e.g., bathroom sink, kitchen, front door).

### ✅ **Why is it effective?**

*   Requires you to physically move away from the bed, reducing the chance of going back to sleep.
*   Registering locations like the bathroom or kitchen can help you naturally start your morning routine.

### 📌 **Recommended for:**

✔ People who tend to go back to bed after turning off their alarm  
✔ Those who want to start their morning routine quickly

## **2. Math Mission** 🧮

![Math Mission](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6748851ec062625f98012b8e/67acb1678cbd11a7ff694f00_1000015184.jpg)

### ✅ **How does it work?**

You must solve a math problem of a chosen difficulty level to turn off the alarm.

### ✅ **Why is it effective?**

*   Activates your brain immediately, reducing drowsiness and improving focus.
*   The difficulty level is adjustable, allowing you to customize your wake-up challenge.

### 📌 **Recommended for:**

✔ People who struggle to wake up with a clear mind  
✔ Those who want to sharpen their focus first thing in the morning

## **3. Shake Mission** 💪

![Shake Mission](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6748851ec062625f98012b8e/67acb1834bd64b9c7952b7e7_1000015192.jpg)

### ✅ **How does it work?**

To turn off the alarm, you need to shake your phone a set number of times.

### ✅ **Why is it effective?**

*   Engages physical movement, helping increase circulation and wake up faster.
*   The number of shakes is adjustable, allowing for a customizable level of physical activity.

### 📌 **Recommended for:**

✔ People who wake up better with movement  
✔ Those looking for a simple physical activity to start their morning

## **4. Barcode/QR Code Mission** 🏷️

![Barcode/QR Code Mission](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6748851ec062625f98012b8e/67acb1a5fe632ec5a3458353_1000015188.jpg)

### ✅ **How does it work?**

You must scan a pre-registered **barcode or QR code** (e.g., toothpaste barcode, a book's QR code) to turn off the alarm.

### ✅ **Why is it effective?**

*   Requires you to move to a specific location, preventing you from staying in bed.
*   Helps establish a consistent morning routine by associating wake-up with a specific action.

### 📌 **Recommended for:**

✔ People who struggle to get out of bed  
✔ Those who prefer structured morning routines

## **5. Memory mission** 🧠

![Memory mission](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6748851ec062625f98012b8e/67acb1bf79c8d1877f4982f6_1000015179.jpg)

### ✅ **How does it work?**

You need to memorize and correctly enter a sequence of numbers displayed on the screen.

### ✅ **Why is it effective?**

*   Forces you to actively use your brain, helping transition into wakefulness.
*   Strengthens memory and cognitive function in the morning.

### 📌 **Recommended for:**

✔ People who wake up feeling mentally foggy  
✔ Those who want to engage their brain early in the day

## **6. Typing Mission** ✍️

![Typing Mission](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6748851ec062625f98012b8e/67acb1db4ac89af1e4a1ecfe_1000015181.jpg)

### ✅ **How does it work?**

To turn off the alarm, you must type a given phrase exactly as it appears on the screen.

### ✅ **Why is it effective?**

*   Engages your hands and mind, stimulating wakefulness.
*   Requires active participation rather than just tapping a button.
*   Helps start the day productively with focused engagement.

### 📌 **Recommended for:**

✔ People who struggle with morning focus  
✔ Those who want a simple but effective brain-activating wake-up method

![Walking mission](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6748851ec062625f98012b8e/67acb1f3837fa6796423feef_1000015186.jpg)

### ✅ **How does it work?**

### ✅ **Why is it effective?**

*   Encourages movement, promoting circulation and reducing drowsiness.
*   Prevents going back to bed by getting you up and moving.

### 📌 **Recommended for:**

✔ People who need movement to wake up fully  
✔ Those who tend to stay in bed too long

## **8. Squat Mission** 🏋️‍

![Squat Mission](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/6748851ec062625f98012b8e/67acb20f77dea5760caecea9_1000015197.jpg)

### ✅ **How does it work?**

To turn off the alarm, you must complete a set number of squats.

### ✅ **Why is it effective?**

*   Provides an effective morning exercise routine.
*   Helps build a habit of incorporating physical activity into your wake-up routine.

### 📌 **Recommended for:**

✔ People who want to wake up with physical activity  
✔ Those looking to incorporate exercise into their mornings

## **Which Wake-Up Mission is the Most Popular?**

Which wake-up mission do Alarmy users prefer the most? According to mission usage data, **Math Mission is the most popular.** While solving math problems early in the morning may sound challenging, many users find it effective for waking up their brains and shaking off sleepiness.

Next in popularity are **Shake Mission and Memory Mission.** Shake Mission helps users wake up by engaging physical movement, while Memory Mission activates cognitive function, making it easier to stay awake.

Meanwhile, **Photo Mission, Barcode Mission, Typing Mission, Squat Mission, and Walking Mission** have smaller user bases but are highly favored by specific groups. For example, **Squat Mission and Walking Mission** are particularly useful for those who want to incorporate light exercise into their morning routine.

Interestingly, **wake-up mission preferences vary by country.** In a future article, we’ll explore how different cultures prefer different wake-up challenges!
