# Ki-Jun Kim (Jun)
**CEO | Partner**

#KeepCalmAndCarryOn #TechGeek

I believe that technological innovations drive the future, so I lead the discovery and investment of technology-based startups. Leveraging real-world development and entrepreneurial experience, I strive to discover pioneering technologies faster than anyone else. At times like a cheerful friend, or a genuine and sincere colleague, I am a partner dedicated to standing by entrepreneurs' sides and growing together.

### Career History
- **2024-Present:** CEO, Kakao Ventures
- **2018-2024:** Vice President, Kakao Ventures
- **2014-2018:** Executive Director, Kakao Ventures
- **2012-2014:** Senior Team Leader, Kakao Ventures
- **2011-2012:** Strategic Planning Office, CJ Holdings
- **2005-2010:** SK Communications
- **2004-2005:** Co-founder, Ideawalks
- **1999-2002:** Engineer, VirtualTek

### Education
- Master of Digital Business Administration, Yonsei University School of Information Systems
- Bachelor of Science in Nuclear Engineering, Seoul National University
