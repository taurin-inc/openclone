## VAP/Value-Up Partners

External industry experts collaborate with Kakao Ventures to support the family's growth. By providing professional talents that are difficult for early-stage companies to internalize, we help families resolve their concerns and challenges.

**Wook Jeong**
Game Changer | Games
Wook Jeong, CEO of Neptune, possesses extensive experience and expertise in the gaming industry. He is dedicated to working with Kakao Ventures' gaming family to create a healthy and enjoyable gaming market.

**Hyo-Taek Kim**
Team Catalyst | Organizational Management
Hyo Taek Kim has served as head of HR at Nexon and led HR planning at Doosan. With additional experience as a management consultant, he is an expert in organizational management.

**Keun-Bong Kwak**
Pathfinder | Product
Keun-Bong Kwak, CEO of 1ZLABS, began as an individual developer and held positions such as CTO, CPO, and CMO at NBT, accumulating experience in over 200 IT-related projects as a Product-led Growth specialist.

**So-yeon Lee**
SEO Consulting | SEO
So-yeon Lee, CEO of Buildable, is a global SEO expert who has been responsible for SEO consulting for companies of various sizes and domains, from large corporations to startups, since 2018.

**Jin-seok hong**
Beauty Marketer | Beauty
Jinseok Hong, CMO of Seorin Company, is a beauty brand expert with extensive experience across the entire lifecycle of beauty brands, from major corporations to indie brands. He collaborates on marketing and distribution strategies for beauty industry players.
