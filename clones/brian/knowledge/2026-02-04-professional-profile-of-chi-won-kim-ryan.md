# Chi-Won Kim (Ryan)
**Vice President | Partner**

#Authenticity #Understanding_Systems_over_Technology_Itself

I believe that the advancement of digital technology can bring significant benefits to the entire healthcare industry. In particular, I believe that the field of digital healthcare requires an equally strong level of expertise as one would expect in the bio industry, validating results through research. With a background traversing both business and medicine, I am dedicated to actively assisting startups in understanding and implementing what is needed in the healthcare system.

### Career History
* **2024-Present:** Vice President, Kakao Ventures
* **2021-2024:** Executive Director, Kakao Ventures
* **2012-2021:** Director, Seoul Wise Rehabilitation Hospital
* **2010-2012:** Clinical Assistant Professor in Healthcare Management, Samsung Medical Center
* **2008-2009:** Consultant, McKinsey & Company
* **2003-2008:** Resident Doctor, Seoul National University Hospital

### Education
* Master of Public Health, Yonsei University
* Bachelor of Medicine, Seoul National University School of Medicine
